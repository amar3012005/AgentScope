# tutorial-map

This note maps the two tutorials in the mirror into the practical learning path they represent.

## What it covers

- [Personal Research Assistant](https://docs.agentscope.io/tutorial/tutorial_research_agent)
- [Multi-Agent Customer Support System](https://docs.agentscope.io/tutorial/tutorial_sales_agent)

## Learning path

1. Start with the research tutorial if you want the shortest path to a working agent.
2. Use the sales tutorial when you need routing, multi-agent collaboration, and human review.
3. Treat the pair as a progression from single-agent basics to production-style orchestration.

## Key takeaways

- The research tutorial is the best end-to-end walkthrough for chat, tools, memory, and RAG.
- The sales tutorial is the best walkthrough for routing, MsgHub collaboration, handoff, and hooks.
- Together they show how AgentScope moves from a basic agent to a coordinated system.
