### Professional Email Blueprint

1. **Subject Line**: Compelling and specific (e.g., "Modular Energy: Solvis Lea Technical Update").
2. **Salutation**: Professional greeting.
3. **The 'Why'**: 1 sentence on why we are reaching out.
4. **Key Specs**: 2-3 bullet points of high-impact evidence.
5. **Value Prop**: Why this matters for their specific needs.
6. **CTA**: A soft but clear next step (meeting, site visit, technical docs).
7. **Sign-off**: Professional closing.
