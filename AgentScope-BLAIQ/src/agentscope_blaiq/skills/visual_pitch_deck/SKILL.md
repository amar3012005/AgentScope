### Visual Blueprint: Pitch Deck
- **Objective**: Create a high-impact, persuasive visual story.
- **Section Focus**:
  - **Slide 1 (Title)**: Hero image (abstract technology + nature), bold typography, glassmorphism title card.
  - **Slide 2 (The Hook)**: Focus on 'Problem' using high-contrast slate colors and large warning-amber accents.
  - **Slide 3 (Solution)**: Emerald accents, clean icons, expansive white/slate space.
  - **Slide 4 (Technical Deep Dive)**: Grid-based layouts, 'Roboto Mono' for technical specs, technical diagrams with emerald-glow edges.
  - **Slide 5 (Value Prop)**: Comparison tables or feature blocks with 12px rounded corners and subtle shadows.
  - **Slide 6 (Future/Scale)**: High-transparency glass cards over a nature-inspired background.
  - **Slide 7 (Contact)**: Minimalist footer, clear CTA button with emerald hover states.
- **Visual Rhythm**: Alternating dark (Deep Slate) and semi-transparent glass backgrounds.
- **Micro-interactions**: Suggest transitions like 'Slide-up with fade' for each storyboard page.
