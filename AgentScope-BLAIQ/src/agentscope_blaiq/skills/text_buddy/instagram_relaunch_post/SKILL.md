# Instagram Relaunch Post

## Purpose
The purpose of this skill is to craft high-conviction Instagram relaunch posts specifically for German B2B brands, such as Solvis GmbH. The posts are designed to effectively communicate the brand's renewed vision, engage the target audience, and drive business objectives through strategic content and calls to action.

## When To Use
Use this skill when a German B2B brand is undergoing a relaunch or rebranding and needs to announce this change on Instagram. This is particularly useful for brands looking to refresh their image, introduce new products or services, or re-engage with their audience in a meaningful way.

## Operating Instructions
1. **Hook Options**: Start with a compelling hook to grab attention. Options include:
   - A provocative question related to the industry.
   - A bold statement about the brand's new direction.
   - A surprising fact or statistic relevant to the brand's offerings.

2. **Caption Structure**:
   - **Introduction**: Briefly introduce the relaunch, highlighting the key changes or new offerings.
   - **Body**: Elaborate on the benefits of the relaunch for the audience. Use bullet points for clarity if necessary.
   - **Conclusion**: Reinforce the brand's commitment to quality and innovation.

3. **CTA Guidance**: End with a clear call to action. Encourage the audience to:
   - Visit the website for more information.
   - Contact the sales team for inquiries.
   - Follow the brand for updates.

4. **Hashtag Strategy**: Use a mix of branded and industry-specific hashtags. Include:
   - #SolvisRelaunch (or relevant brand-specific hashtag)
   - #InnovationInEnergy (or industry-specific hashtag)
   - #B2BExcellence

5. **Tone Rules**: Maintain a concise and professional tone. Ensure all language aligns with the brand's voice, emphasizing trust, expertise, and innovation.

## Output Requirements
- The post must be between 150-200 words.
- Include at least three hashtags.
- Ensure the tone is consistent with a professional B2B brand.
- The post should be written in German, reflecting the target audience's language.

## Constraints
- Avoid using overly casual language or slang.
- Do not include any unverified claims about the brand or its products.
- Ensure all content complies with Instagram's community guidelines.

## Examples

**Example 1:**
"Bereit für die Zukunft? 🌟 Solvis GmbH startet neu durch! Mit innovativen Lösungen setzen wir neue Maßstäbe in der Energiebranche. Entdecken Sie, wie unsere neuesten Technologien Ihr Unternehmen effizienter machen können. Besuchen Sie unsere Website für mehr Informationen. #SolvisRelaunch #InnovationInEnergy #B2BExcellence"

**Example 2:**
